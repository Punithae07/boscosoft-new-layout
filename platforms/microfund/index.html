FEATURES

Microfinance & Credit Modules

Streamline loan processing, agent collections, and branch accounting for microfinance groups.

Group & Center Management
Group borrowers into Centers and Joint Liability Groups (JLG). Coordinate meeting schedules, attendance, and joint liability rules.
Loan Portfolio Management
Configure custom loan products with declining or flat interest rates, processing fees, insurance charges, and repayment frequencies.
Agent Field App
Equip field agents with an offline Android app to log cash receipts, verify balances, and print Bluetooth receipts on-site.
Risk & Portfolio Quality
Monitor Portfolio at Risk (PAR) matrixes, track late repayment alerts, and run credit score analyses.

ADVANTAGES

Why Choose MicroFund?

Offline-First Mobile App

Collect loan payments in deep rural regions without internet. Data syncs automatically once agents return to network range.

Bluetooth Printer Support

Flexible Repayment Schedules
